This is the mini version of the Arduinosound Turbo, without stereo sampling and an analog preamp

ICs:
Arduino Nano

Capacitors:
220nF	1	
10uF	1	

Resistors:
220Ω	8	
47Ω	1	
100K	2	

Connectors:
2.1mm DC barrel jack (for power) 1
1/4" phono jack or RCA jack (for audio input) 1
DB25 male (for parallel port) 1
